About Ben Tennyson

Ben Tennyson is a young hero who discovers the powerful Omnitrix, a device that allows him to transform into different alien species.

Heatblast

A fire-based alien with the ability to shoot flames and control heat.

Four Arms

A powerful alien with four arms and super strength.

XLR8

A super-fast alien capable of incredible speed and agility.

The Omnitrix

The Omnitrix is an advanced alien device that stores DNA samples from different alien species, allowing transformation.

Fun Fact

The Omnitrix was created by an alien genius to promote peace across the universe.
